Meet the trainer of this course !

James Schomburgk is the owner of The Second Visit, an international clinical education company, and a lecturer in Sports and Orthopaedic Medicine at the University of Adelaide. About Us

Meet Jatinbir Singh, a seasoned musculoskeletal physiotherapist with over 6 years of experience in Australia. He holds a master's degree in Advanced Clinical Physiotherapy from the University of South Australia and is passionate about promoting clinical excellence and international collaboration. Jatinbir is the founder of Physiomates, an online platform that helps international physiotherapists understand the registration and practice requirements in Australia. Through resources, guidance, and support, Physiomates empowers aspiring physiotherapists to pursue their careers in Australia.

Beyond Physiomates, Jatinbir manages two online communities with over 6,000 international physiotherapists, providing a space for knowledge exchange and professional networking. He's also committed to continuing education, organizing a clinical course in India with esteemed Australian speaker James Schomborgh. Join Jatinbir Singh and Physiomates in advancing physiotherapy practice and shaping the future of the profession. Let's unlock the potential of physiotherapy and inspire excellence worldwide.
