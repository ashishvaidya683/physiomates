Meet the trainer of this course !

James Schomburgk, the founder of The Second Visit, brings a wealth of expertise to the field of Physiotherapy. As a lecturer in Sports and Orthopaedic Medicine at the University of Adelaide's School of Medicine, his commitment to advancing healthcare education is evident.

With a distinguished career, including directorship roles at Back in Motion Campbelltown and Valley View, James has been recognized for his inspiring leadership, receiving the 'Most Inspiring Director' award in 2017. His extensive experience as the State SA Director of Back in Motion and as the WorkCover South Australia Physio Advisor for 8 years underscores his profound impact on the profession.

An esteemed advisor to the Australian Health Professional Regulation Authority and the National Chairman of MPA (Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy Australia), James remains actively engaged in shaping the future of physiotherapy. With over a decade of teaching experience at the University of South Australia and as a guest lecturer at Flinders University, his dedication to educating the next generation of physiotherapists is unwavering.

Organizer and Speaker

  • Meet Jatinbir Singh, originally from Chandigarh, India a seasoned Musculoskeletal and Sports Physiotherapist with over 6 years of experience in Australia. Currently a Clinical Lead Physiotherapist at a prominent Musculoskeletal and Sports Injury Clinic in Melbourne. He holds a master's degree in Advanced Clinical Physiotherapy from the University of South Australia and is passionate about promoting clinical excellence and international collaboration. Jatinbir is the founder of Physiomates, an online platform that helps international physiotherapists understand the registration and practice requirements in Australia. Through resources, guidance, and support, Physiomates empowers aspiring physiotherapists to pursue their careers in Australia.
  • Beyond Physiomates, Jatinbir manages two online communities with over 6,000 international physiotherapists, providing a space for knowledge exchange and professional networking. He's also committed to continuing education, organizing a clinical course in India with esteemed Australian speaker James Schomborgh.
